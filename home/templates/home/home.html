About Gen254

Gen254 is a comprehensive platform designed to empower citizens and promote transparency in governance. Our mission is to provide an intuitive and user-friendly interface for various civic engagement activities:

  • Case Reporting: Easily submit and document cases of corruption, misconduct, or other issues affecting your community.
  • Case Tracking: Stay informed about the progress of your reported cases through our real-time tracking system.
  • Government Project Monitoring: Access information about government-launched projects, including their current status - whether stalled, in-progress, or completed.
  • Transparency Portal: View and analyze data on various cases and projects to promote accountability.

By bridging the gap between citizens and authorities, Gen254 aims to foster a more transparent, responsive, and efficient governance system. Join us in building a better future for Kenya!

Our Services
